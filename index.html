Industrial Integration with a Legacy

Real-world plant experience, tough installs, and clean solutions — built to run and built to last.

For over 25 years, Southwest Systems Integrators has delivered real-world solutions in material handling, automation, and mechanical contracting. From small-shop installs to Fortune 500 integrations—we build systems that last.

Built from ironworker bloodlines and field-earned trust, our lineage reaches back through generations of hands-on problem solvers. Whether we're designing a custom conveyor retrofit or sourcing critical hardware, our focus has never changed: Make it work. Make it right. Make it last.

Today, we operate with the same grit and precision that made us the go-to name for companies like Kraft, Amazon, and PepsiCo—bringing clarity to complexity, one system at a time.

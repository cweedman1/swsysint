Our Story

Who We Are, What We Do, and Why It Works

Thank you for visiting our website. While you’re here, we want to share who we are, what we do, and why Southwest Systems Integrators has remained a trusted partner for more than 25 years.

We are systems integrators with an extensive background in material handling systems, industrial equipment, and general contracting. Our experience spans a wide range of industries and applications — from food and beverage packaging conveyors to refuse handling systems, warehousing automation, overhead chain conveyors, and custom machine fabrication.

Beyond equipment and installation, we provide complete project management services. That includes system design, equipment sourcing, installation, maintenance support, and site preparation or modification. We don’t just install systems — we take responsibility for making them work in the real world.

As manufacturer representatives, we are able to coordinate new equipment purchases efficiently and objectively. We also serve as exclusive distributors for select product lines, allowing us to support our clients from initial concept through long-term operation.

Our driving principle is simple: your satisfaction. We aim to earn repeat customers by being the one resource you can rely on — whether the project is large or small, new or existing, planned or urgent.

We believe long-term success is built on trust, accountability, and delivering systems that perform as promised. That philosophy has guided our work for decades, and it continues to define every project we take on today.

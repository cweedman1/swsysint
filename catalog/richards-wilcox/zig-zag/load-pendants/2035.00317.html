Rigid Straight Side Load Pendant 75 LB Capacity

The Rigid Straight Side Pendant is similar to the Rigid Clevis Pendant except the sides are straight. It may be spaced on multiples of 6". It attaches directly to the load pin on the vertical link of the
conveyor chain and is furnished in a zincplated finish. This pendant may be used where a
swinging load may be objectionable or not permitted. The pendant always remains perpendicular to the track.
